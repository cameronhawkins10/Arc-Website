Founded in Faith

Inspired by Genesis 6:14, Arc Learning Academy was founded with a commitment to innovative education. The name reflects the biblical narrative of Noah's ark and symbolizes a promise to families—a commitment to creative, functional, and enjoyable education. Arc Learning Academy strives to be a beacon of educational excellence, offering parents and students a new path forward in partnership and faith. With a vision to provide an alternative option for children's education, the founders embarked on a journey guided by their faith, trusting that God would provide. Their dedication to this mission echoes Noah's obedience to God's instructions, even in the face of uncertainty. Just as Noah built an ark, Arc Learning Academy aims to provide a safe and nurturing environment where children can thrive academically and spiritually.

Our Vision

Arc Learning Academy envisions providing families with a transformative alternative to traditional public school education. We are committed to educating each student based on their unique needs and interests through innovative, research-backed approaches. Our flexible structure fosters a sense of community while maintaining the benefits of traditional schooling. Rooted in a Christian worldview, we offer students opportunities to explore biblical connections in their studies. Our goal is to empower children to become self-advocates and pave their own paths into the future.

Our Mission

The mission of Arc Learning Academy is to cultivate the holistic development of each child through personalized education plans. We emphasize social skills, encourage a closer relationship with Jesus, and incorporate real-life and vocational skills. Our curriculum covers essential content areas such as reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies, ensuring a well-rounded education that prepares students for life beyond the classroom.
